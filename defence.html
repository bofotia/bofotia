RADAR

Leader in design and development of rugged, high-performance RADAR Electronics and sub-systems for Data Acquisition & Signal Processing systems like RPUs, Transmit-Receive Modules (TRMs), Receivers, Synthesizers and Data Recorders.

We provide comprehensive design, development and integration services for RADAR electronics subsystems, from initial prototyping through low volume production with the agility to adapt to changing customer requirements throughout the lifecycle. These services are complemented by our global partnership with leading organizations specializing in defence electronics COTS like Curtiss Wright Defence, Elma Electronics and Wind River Systems. Our RADAR systems are used in a wide range of commercial, military and paramilitary systems including ground-based, ship and airborne radar systems. Many of the RADAR Electronics and RADAR sub-systems designed by us have been deployed in a wide range of defence and aerospace applications including ground-based, ship and airborne radar systems.

Our RADAR sub-systems utilize a broad range of technologies including mixed signal, high-speed digital, radio frequency (RF) and specialized electro-optics (EO). Our RADAR subsystems designs are backed by in-house research and development operations that help ensure that the solutions offered incorporate and remain at the leading edge of signal processing technology.

SONAR

Design and development of rugged, high-performance SONAR Sub-systems like underwater transducers, front-end signal conditioning units, signal processors, and display systems. AXISCADES has wide experience in designing SONAR Electronics and sub-systems for data acquisition and signal processing in various SONAR Systems like Diver detection, Coastal Surveillance, CIS/CMS etc.

Our global partnerships are with leading organizations specializing in defence COTS like Curtiss Wright Defence and Wind River Systems and a strong hardware design and software development team, and can provide custom solutions for various SONAR systems and subsystems like: Transmitters, Receivers, Beam Forming, Fire Control System (FCS) and Multi-function Displays.These systems can be implemented for multiple platforms like submarines, carriers, fighters, frigates etc.

We work closely with leading-edge defence R&D community in India has been actively involved is design and development of these indigenous SONAR systems implemented by the India Defence. We are certified by CEMILAC for testing and validation for defence electronics and all our designs ensure environmental compliance and EMI/EMC as per JSS55555, MIL STD 810D/G and MIL STD 461E.

ELECTRONIC WARFARE

Extensive experience in implementing a wide array of Electronic Warfare solutions including: Friend or Foe Identification (IFF), Electronic Support Measures (ESM) and Electronic Counter Measures (ECM). AXISCADES has extensive experience in implementing a wide array of electronic warfare solutions like friend or foe identification (IFF), electronic support measures (ESM) and electronic counter measures (ECM), Digital RF Memory subsystems and wideband jammers. The ESM and ECM systems include gathering of EW information through Electronic Intelligence (ELINT), Communications Intelligence (COMINT) and other ESM receivers.

Our engineers can help specify, develop, integrate, test and support leading-edge Electronic Warfare solutions by implementing signal processing algorithms on DSP or FPGA platforms with DO-178B compliant software implementation, testing and qualification, operational support, and system upgrades.
