Aircraft Interiors, Flight Test Instrumentation, Test Rigs & Panel Drawings
Creation of build list (BOM) for all wiring diagrams
Layout design / Harness Routing Maps
Advanced Material Ordering (AMO) Creation
Change management (PCR/RFC/NIEO)

2D Wiring diagrams & ESSDs.
Advanced Material Ordering (AMO)

Back shell Selection / Define Backshell clocking angle

Grounding and Bonding compliance
Harness Release Process in Enovia LCA and Datalink / BDI
