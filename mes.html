Because every MES solution should be based on your individual intent, purpose and function. An effective MES system needs to be customized for your industry and more importantly, your specific needs. We can help you with that.

With a structured phase approach of understanding your current framework at different levels, we’ll suggest the right MES solution that will increase operational efficiency.

Integrate your CAD, PLM, Shop Floor, ERP, 3rd party and data from legacy systems and create a data rich environment that is accurate and provides a real time view.

With our industry experts supporting you through the entire process, effectively manage your implementation and pick and choose the delivery model that fits your need.

We’ll help you conduct regular benefit evaluations to ensure your goals are being met and the deployment is successful.

Getting your enterprise Industry 4.0 ready

A smart factory that’s industry 4.0 ready needs expertise in all levels of evolution related to PLC, Process control systems, SCADA and ERP. AXISCADES brings you a total MES solution, support your hardware and software implementation framework and use real-time collected data from multiple systems and framework for effective production performance.

  • Product lifecycle management
  • Enterprise Resource planning
  • Customer relationship management
  • Human resource management
  • Production information management system
  • Warehouse management system
  • Maintenance management systems
  • Supervisory control and data acquisition
  • Programmable logic controllers
  • Distributed control systems
  • Automation systems

With our Shop-floor MES support, all the modules are integrated to create one platform view (paperless information) that in-turn creates manufacturing excellence.

Production

Scheduling, Job tracking, downtime, recording production quantity, record scarp production, OEE, Log book tracking for resource management & model execution of manufacturing process.

Quality

Quality management software, SPC, Process monitoring, link to latest or updated documents, resolution of non-conformances, customer and supplier complaints.

Inventory operations

Inventory management, build traceability of parts, warehouse-logistics.

Maintenance

Asset management, optimize maintenance activities, Repairs (speed-up response break-down), tool management and energy management.
