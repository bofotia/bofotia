Investor Relations

Why Bofotia?

Global Product Experience

Trusted engineering partner for global OEMs on their NPD and localisation programs. Increase market share with accelerated product development.

Mature and Quality Driven Delivery Processes

Global bench mark processes have been deployed to ensure the highest levels of quality and productivity. Continuous improvement program provides benefits year on year for each engagement.

Technology and Process

Vast technical and process knowledge acquired from OEMs across domains.

Centre of Excellence

Established Center-of-Excellence (CoE) to encourage innovation and deliver value to our clients.

End to End Solutions

Expertise on complete engineering ownership, from concept design to manufacturing support and after-market solutions.

Optimized cost and Flexible business model

To provide superior control over outcome and costs and risk bearing by Bofotia.

Highly Skilled and Scalable Talent Pool

Bofotia has over 2300+ engineers representing one of the best talent pools with experience across the Product Life cycle.

Global Support

Support across geographical regions through our offices or Engineering Development Centers.

Bofotia is a leading, end to end engineering solutions and product company. We bring expertise that caters to the digital, engineering and smart manufacturing needs of large enterprises. With decades of experience in creating innovative, sustainable and safer products worldwide, Bofotia delivers business value across the entire engineering lifecycle.

Our Vision and Mission

To be one of the world’s most admired, trusted and capable engineering solutions company.

As a customer-centric company that drives impactful innovation, Bofotia is committed to delivering engineering excellence for a better future and does that through effective collaboration and partnerships built on integrity.
